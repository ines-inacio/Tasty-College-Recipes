Bacon and Mushroom Lasagna

Preparation and Cooking Time:

2h30m

Servings:

15

Total Price:

23€

Ingredients

  • 16 lasagna pasta sheets
  • 3 teaspoons of salt
  • 4 teaspoons of olive oil
  • 800g of ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 teaspoons of black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/4 cup of white wine
  • 1 pack of lasagna 'Knorr'
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 200g of laminated mushrooms
  • 250g of thick bacon strips
  • 500ml of béchamel sauce
  • 120g of grated cheddar cheese
  • 120g of grated gouda cheese
  • 120g of grated mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Fill a large pot with water, and 1 teaspoon of salt and let it boil on high heat.
  2. Cook only 2 to 3 pasta sheets at a time - to avoid them sticking to each other - for about 5 minutes.
  3. Let each pasta sheet cool separately.
  4. In a separate pot, add the minced onion and garlic, 4 teaspoons of olive oil, and 1 teaspoon of salt and stir for about 3 minutes on medium heat.
  5. Add the laminated mushrooms and the bacon strips to the mix and let it cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Preheat the oven to 180ºC.
  7. Add the ground beef into the pot and season it with the black pepper, the paprika, and 1 teaspoon of salt and let it simmer, while stirring occasionally, until the beef is fully cooked.
  8. Add the lasagna 'Knorr' and the white wine to the beef and stir completely.
  9. Add 1/4 cup of water to the beef and 'Knorr' and turn the heat off.
  10. In a baking dish, gently place 4 of the lasagna sheets and pour some of the premade béchamel sauce covering each sheet.
  11. Spread the beef, mushroom and bacon mix evenly over the 4 lasagna sheets.
  12. Sprinkle some of the cheddar, gouda, and mozzarella cheese over the beef and lasagna sheets.
  13. Cover everything with another 4 lasagna sheets.
  14. Repeat steps 11 to 13 until you run out of lasagna sheets.
  15. Pour the remainder of the béchamel sauce, covering all of the lasagna.
  16. Lastly, sprinkle the lasagna with the remainder of the grated cheeses and place the baking dish in the oven for about 30 minutes or until the cheese is completely melted.
  17. Cut the lasagna and it's ready to serve!
