Creamy Farfalle with Tuna

Preparation and Cooking Time:

0h30m

Servings:

4

12â‚¬

Ingredients

  • 500g of farfalle pasta
  • 235g of tuna (about 3 cans)
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 4 teaspoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 25g of parsley
  • 300ml of white sauce

Directions

  1. Fill a large pot with water, 1 teaspoon of salt, and 2 teaspoons of olive oil and let it boil on high heat for about 7 minutes.
  2. Once the water boils, place the farfalle pasta in the pot.
  3. Add the minced onion and garlic, as well as 1 teaspoon of salt, and 2 teaspoons of olive oil into a frying pan on medium heat and stir for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the white sauce, the black pepper, and the chopped parsley in the frying pan and stir everything, letting it simmer.
  5. Add the tuna to the frying pan and stir.
  6. Once the farfalle pasta is fully cooked, pour the water out and add the mix in the frying pan to the pasta in the large pot.
  7. Stir everything until the farfalle pasta is completely covered with the sauce.
  8. Serve it while it's hot!
