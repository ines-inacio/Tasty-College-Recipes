Colorful Spaghetti

Preparation and Cooking Time:

0h40m

Servings:

6

Ingredients

  • 500g of spaghetti
  • 3 teaspoons of salt
  • 360g of sausages (about 8 long sausages)
  • 500g of brocolli
  • 500g of julienne carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 4 teaspoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/4 cup of white wine
  • 200ml of white sauce

Directions

  1. Fill a large pot with water, 1 teaspoon of salt, and 2 teaspoons of olive oil and let it boil on high heat for about 7 minutes.
  2. Once the water boils, place the spaghetti in the pot.
  3. Add the minced onion and garlic, as well as 1 teaspoon of salt, 2 teaspoons of olive oil, and the cut up sausages in a large frying pan on medium heat.
  4. Fill another pot with water and let it boil on high heat for about 7 minutes.
  5. Once the water boils, place the brocolli in the pot and turn the heat to medium.
  6. Place the julienne carrots in the frying pan and stir for 2 minutes.
  7. Once the brocolli are cooked, add them to the frying pan with the white wine and stir everything.
  8. When the spaghetti is fully cooked, pour the water out.
  9. Add the white sauce, and the black pepper onto the mix in the frying pan and keep stiring for about 5 minutes.
  10. Add the mix in the large pot with the spaghetti and stir.
  11. Now, it's ready to serve!
